Surfing Waves

Surfing is a popular water sport that involves riding ocean waves on a surfboard. Surfers can ride waves that are breaking to the left or right, or waves that are breaking in both directions. The best waves for surfing are typically found in the ocean, but some surfers also ride waves on lakes and rivers.
